DAO stands for Decentralized Autonomous Organization. Without going into the semantics of the term DAO, it is a community of people who make decentralized decisions and become autonomous through their software underpinning. A DAO can be viewed as a tool that can be used by communities and organizations of various types. This blog will look into different DAO frameworks and opportunities to apply it to legacy organizations.
